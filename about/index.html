About

Hi Internet,

this is Vlad writing and I welcome you to my corner of Internet where I share stuff about Software Engineering, Computer Science, and life in general.

I’m not that big on talking about myself, so I’m just gonna keep it short and to the point.

Q: Who are you, Vlad?

A: Just a man on this planet, currently living in Spain and working as a Software Engineer. I love learning new things and challenging myself with new things, especially when it comes to Software Engineering and Computer Science. I also like travelling and exploring new places. Check my LinkedIn profile should you want to learn more about the professional Vlad. Feel free to also have a look at the GitHub profile should you be interested into my commits.

A: I always wanted to have one, but I never had the time, or the inspiration, to put one together and keep it up to date. Because I do not give up that easily, I said to myself it was time to give it (yet) another chance.

Q: What will you be blogging about?

A: Mainly about Software Engineering / Computer Science and should the muses be on my side about life and co.

Q: How can people get in touch with you?

A: The best way is over LinkedIn or Twitter. You could also drop me an email at flore.vlad@gmail.com.
