About

Hi Internet,

I’m Vlad — welcome to my little corner of the web where I share thoughts on Software Engineering, Computer Science, and life in general.

Want to know more about my professional background? Check out my LinkedIn. Interested in what I build or tinker with? Visit my GitHub.

Q: How can people reach you?

A: You can find me on LinkedIn, or drop me an email at flore.vlad@gmail.com.

Thanks for stopping by — and happy reading!
